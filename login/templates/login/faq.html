FAQ Pheonix Travel & Tour Frequently Asked Questions




This section contains a wealth of information, related to Pheonix Travel & Tour . If you cannot find an answer to your question, make sure to contact us.

General questions

Is account registration required?

Account registration at is only required if the customer is going to book a flight ticket or wants to chat with support agent.

Can I request for a new destination to be added to the list ?

Yes, you can write to us, based on popular demand appropriate modifications can be added by us.

Can I cancel a ticket that has been booked ?

Yes ticket cancellation within 24hrs of booking for registered customers is entitled for a full refund and for premium customers it is available till a day vefore the scheduled travel date.
Customers

Can I carry extra baggage items?

Yes!, it would be subject to extra charges beyond 10kg for domestic and beyond 18kg for international travel.

How to upgrade to a premium account?

The steps involved in this process are really simple. All you need to do is:
  • Register an account
  • Activate your account
  • Go to the Home section
  • Select upgrade to premium.

What are the discounts available?

Various disv=counts are offered periodically and it automatically gets applied at the time of ticket booking.

Can I edit my travel date after booking the ticket?

Yes, it can be done till 24 hrs prior to travel date, but it would be subject to extra charges.

What are the payment options?

Online banking, Credit/Debit cards and Cash are the various payment modes accepted.

How do I check my ticket status?

Visit My Bookings tab under home, You would be able to see your booking info and status.
